More About Us.

Kindred Courier Services is a full service intra-country courier operator serving Kenya. We deliver a new approach to business in Kenya, which is more current with worldwide standards. Our foremost goal is to ensure that our services meet the needs of every client, on time , every time. For many , a lot of time is wasted waiting on line to pay bills or renew licenses. Receiving mail or packages can take days, and many businesses are challenged with poor communication and organization. Kindred Courier Services was formed to address these needs. By offering services that are in line with global standards, we hope to modernize the mode of business and life in Kenya.

The entire Kindred Courier Services staff is trained to master our services goals in three areas: speed, professionalism and reliability. All of our couriers are carefully screened and trained. Each courier is uniformed, courteous and professional making them easily identified. Our customers service department is always available to answer questions, coordinate orders or to take your suggestions to improve and broaden our services. Our experienced and dedicated staff upholds our commitment to providing prompt and worry-free services. Not only do our standards allow us to provide you with outstanding services, but it also differentiates us from existing services in Kenya.

Kindred Courier Services has designed a wide range of services to address the everyday needs of businesses and residents in Nairobi and other towns. Same-day delivery services include messenger services, mail runs, bill payments, bank deposits, legal/financial documents handling, large load transport and distribution services. We guarantee that your pick-up and delivery orders will be expedited on schedule. Our free PAPOHAPO™ service keeps clients worry-free with instant delivery notification. Our superior service is offered at fair, direct and competitive price. It is our desire to make our services as informative and easy to use as possible.
